{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead color="primary" %}} TransHub is a grass-root, volunteer-run initiative, working with **therapeutic education** and harm reduction, in the framework of medical transitions. Our aim is to inform the trans community of their healthcare alternatives, following the therapeutic education idea that people need to **reappropriate the means of their healthcare.** We believe that precise and well-sourced knowledge on all possible treatments is key to **seize back the means of our medical transition, and to reduce harm.** {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{% blocks/lead color="secondary" %}} **TFSF - Transfeministisk Solidaritetsförbund** TransHub is the ressources, information, and action platform operated by TFSF - Transfeministisk Solidaritetsförbund. TFSF is an organization promoting therapeutic education and harm reduction for transgender people.
